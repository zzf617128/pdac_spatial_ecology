# Cross-Platform Evidence Summary

This document summarizes the orthogonal-validation evidence archived in the enhanced lock. It is a provenance summary only and does not modify ED10, source data or manuscript claims.

## Summary

The final enhanced evidence layer combines two GeoMx datasets and one CosMx dataset in ED10 v1. GSE240078 and GSE199102 provide independent compartment-level support for CAF/matrix and immune/TME programs. GSE310352 provides slide/FOV-level support for CAF/matrix-associated TGF/EMT stromal-interface organization. Cho IMC Zenodo 15596960 is retained as a source-only protein-level context dataset, and vascular-niche IMC Zenodo 10246315 remains internal feasibility only.

## Claim Scope

- ED10 v1 supports orthogonal compartment-level and stromal-interface plausibility.
- GSE310352 remains slide/FOV-level because patient/specimen/tissue-block identifiers could not be reliably recovered from public processed-slide metadata.
- GSE310352 cell states are rule-based because author cell-type annotations were unavailable.
- TGF/EMT is interpreted as stromal-interface, not tumor-intrinsic EMT.
- No causal signaling, direct SPP1-CD44 validation, Visium gradient reconstruction or lymph-node immune-uncoupling validation is claimed.
