# ED10 v1 Methods Draft

Extended Data Fig. 10 was generated from completed orthogonal-validation output tables without modifying manuscript files or prior figure panels. For GSE240078 GeoMx DSP, module-level TME AOI versus carcinoma AOI deltas were read from the existing normalized module-test table. For GSE199102 GeoMx WTA, patient-level median TME-like deltas were computed by comparing CAF plus Immune segments with epithelial segments; paired ROI support used CAF-minus-epithelial paired segment results. Module scores had been computed by z-scoring available genes across segments and averaging genes within each module. Cross-GeoMx concordance used the sign of the GSE240078 TME-minus-carcinoma delta and the GSE199102 TME-like-minus-epithelial delta. For GSE310352 CosMx analyses, public metadata allowed mapping of processed slides to GSM sample accessions and confirmed treatment-naive PDAC resection/FFPE tissue context, but did not provide reliable patient, specimen or tissue-block identifiers for the processed slide files. Accordingly, CosMx spatial-adjacency analyses were summarized at the slide and FOV levels and were not interpreted as patient-level validation. Slide-level CAF/matrix to TGF/EMT adjacency log2 observed/expected values and robustness summaries were read from the finalized robustness tables. Source data for all plotted quantities were exported with panel, dataset, module, metric and note fields.
