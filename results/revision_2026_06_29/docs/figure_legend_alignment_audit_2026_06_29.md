## Purpose

The Results section was reorganized into four story modules. This audit records the corresponding figure-legend cleanup so that the manuscript, main figures, Extended Data figures and supplementary tables now carry the same evidence hierarchy.

## Claim-boundary checks

- Figure 1 preserves observational language and does not claim causality, mature TLS formation or clinical prediction.
- Figure 2 frames lymph-node immune decoupling as a five-sample, hypothesis-generating lead.
- Figure 3 frames matrix-integrin, SPP1-CD44/integrin and TGF-beta/TGFBR as perturbation-priority axes, not proven causal signaling.
- Figure 4 explicitly states that GSE274557 does not validate lymph-node-specific immune decoupling and that GSE274673 does not establish direct CAF-to-tumor epithelial proximity.
